How to play

  • Spin the wheel. Each spin lands on a real nation at a specific World Cup — Brazil 1970, Hungary 1954, Argentina 2022…
  • Draft one player per spin. You hold 2 re-spins to reject a squad you don't fancy.
  • Chemistry counts. Teammates from the same World Cup squad link up and boost your team.
  • Name a captain, then play the tournament live: three group games, a knockout draw, extra time, penalty shootouts.
  • The perfect run is seven wins inside normal time: 7-0.
    • 7-0 is an independent, fan-made game inspired by 38-0.app and 82-0.com. Not affiliated with FIFA or any federation. Player ratings are an unofficial, descriptive interpretation.
