# 7-0 — Build the Ultimate World Cup XI

A free, fan-made World Cup draft game. Spin the wheel, draft legends from **43 iconic World Cup squads (1954–2022)**, pick your formation and captain, then live out the whole tournament minute by minute — three group games, a knockout draw, extra time, penalty shootouts.

**The perfect run is seven wins inside normal time: 7-0.**

## Play

🎮 **[Play it live](https://seven-zero-navy.vercel.app)**

## How it works

- 🎡 **Spin the wheel** — each spin lands on a real nation at a specific World Cup: Brazil 1970, Hungary 1954, Argentina 2022…
- ⚽ **Draft one player per spin** into your chosen formation (4-3-3, 4-4-2, 4-2-3-1, 3-5-2). You hold 2 re-spins.
- 🔗 **Chemistry counts** — teammates from the same World Cup squad link up and boost your side.
- 👑 **Name a captain**, then play the tournament live with commentary, group tables, extra time and shootouts.
- 🟩 **Share your run** — emoji result grid, career trophy cabinet saved locally.

## Modes

| Mode | Twist |
|------|-------|
| Classic | Standard opponents |
| Hard | Player ratings hidden |
| Legend | Brutal draw + hidden ratings |

## Tech

Single self-contained `index.html` — vanilla JS and CSS, zero dependencies, no build step. 497 players across 43 squads, Poisson-based match engine, localStorage career stats.

## Run locally

Open `index.html` in a browser. That's it.
