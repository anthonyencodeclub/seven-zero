How to play

  • Spin the wheel. Each spin lands on a real nation at a specific World Cup — Brazil 1970, Hungary 1954, Argentina 2022…
  • Draft one player per spin and place them anywhere — you pick the exact position. You hold one re-spin to reject a squad you don't fancy.
  • Out of position costs. One line out of place −4 rating, two lines −9, and anything involving the goalkeeper −15. A striker in goal is your call…
  • Chemistry is history. Players bond if they actually played together at a World Cup, share a national shirt, or come from the same era or continent — and natural positions earn ⚡ too.
  • Name a captain, then play the tournament live: tactics at half-time, extra time, penalty shootouts — and the occasional line of very famous commentary.
  • The perfect run is seven wins inside normal time: 7-0. Every finished run is saved to the world rankings automatically.
    • 7-0 is an independent, fan-made game inspired by 38-0.app and 82-0.com. Not affiliated with FIFA or any federation. Player ratings are an unofficial, descriptive interpretation.
